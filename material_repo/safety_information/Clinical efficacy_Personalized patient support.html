Safety Information for Oncozen

As a healthcare professional, it's essential to be aware of the safety profile of Oncozen to provide personalized patient support.

  • ● Common Adverse Reactions:
    • Neutropenia
    • Fatigue
    • Nausea
  • ● Serious Adverse Reactions:
    • Severe neutropenia
    • Febrile neutropenia
    • Cardiac toxicity
