Safety Information

At Oncozen, we are committed to continuous innovation in the development of our oncology treatment. As part of this commitment, we prioritize the safety and well-being of patients.

  • Adverse Reactions: The most common adverse reactions reported in clinical trials were fatigue, nausea, and diarrhea.
  • Contraindications: Oncozen is contraindicated in patients with severe hypersensitivity to any component of the product.
  • Warnings and Precautions: Monitor patients for signs of cardiac toxicity and hepatic impairment.

For a comprehensive overview of Oncozen's safety profile, please refer to the full Prescribing Information.
