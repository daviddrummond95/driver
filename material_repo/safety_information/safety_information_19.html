Safety Information: Oncozen for Pediatric Cancer

Dear Healthcare Professional,

We want to provide you with important safety information regarding Oncozen for pediatric cancer patients:

  • Oncozen is indicated for patients aged 2-18 years with refractory or relapsed solid tumors.
  • Common side effects include fatigue, nausea, and decreased appetite. Monitor patients closely for these symptoms.
  • Regular blood tests are required to monitor hematological parameters during treatment.
  • Dose adjustments may be necessary based on individual patient response and tolerability.

Please refer to the full prescribing information for complete safety details. Report any adverse events to our pharmacovigilance department.

Important Safety Notice:

Oncozen may increase the risk of infections. Advise patients and caregivers to report any signs of infection promptly.

For additional information or to report side effects, please contact our medical information department.
