Safety Information

Oncozen has a robust safety profile, with a low incidence of severe adverse events.

  • In clinical trials, the most common adverse events were mild to moderate and reversible.
  • Oncozen demonstrated a low risk of severe neutropenia, anemia, and thrombocytopenia compared to other oncology treatments.
  • No cumulative toxicity was observed, and dose reductions or interruptions were rarely necessary.

The safety profile of Oncozen supports its use in a variety of clinical settings, including patients with comorbidities and those who have experienced treatment-related toxicities.
