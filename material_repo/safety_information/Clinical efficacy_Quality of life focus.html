Safety Information

As you consider Oncozen for your patients, it's essential to understand the safety profile of this oncology drug.

  • Common Adverse Reactions:
    • Nausea and vomiting
    • Fatigue
    • Diarrhea
  • Serious Adverse Reactions:
    • Neutropenia
    • Anemia
    • Thrombocytopenia

Oncozen is committed to improving the quality of life for patients with cancer. While safety is a top priority, it's essential to weigh the benefits of treatment against the potential risks.

For more information on the safety profile of Oncozen, please refer to the full prescribing information.
