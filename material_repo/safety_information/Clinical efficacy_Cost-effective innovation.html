Safety Information

As a healthcare professional, it's essential to understand the safety profile of Oncozen, a cost-effective innovation in oncology treatment.

  • Oncozen has been shown to have a favorable safety profile in clinical trials, with a low incidence of severe adverse events.
  • The most common adverse events reported were mild to moderate and reversible, with no unexpected safety signals identified.
