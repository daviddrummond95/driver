Safety Information

As a healthcare professional, it's essential to understand the safety profile of Oncozen, an innovative oncology drug that targets cancer cells through its unique mechanism of action.

  • Oncozen has been shown to have a favorable safety profile in clinical trials, with a low incidence of severe adverse events.
  • The most common adverse reactions reported in clinical trials were mild to moderate and included nausea, fatigue, and diarrhea.
  • Oncozen has not been associated with any significant increases in serious adverse events, including cardiovascular events or secondary malignancies.

As with any cancer therapy, it's crucial to carefully review the full prescribing information and discuss the benefits and risks of Oncozen with your patients.
