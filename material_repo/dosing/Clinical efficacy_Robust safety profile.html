Optimal Dosing for Effective Treatment with Oncozen

As a healthcare professional, you understand the importance of balancing efficacy with safety when treating patients with cancer. Oncozen is designed to provide a robust safety profile while delivering clinical efficacy.

Dosing Regimen Recommended Dose
Initial Dose 2 mg/m² IV infusion over 1 hour
Subsequent Doses 1.5 mg/m² IV infusion over 1 hour, every 3 weeks

Oncozen's dosing regimen is designed to minimize adverse reactions while maintaining its anti-tumor activity. By following the recommended dosing schedule, you can help your patients achieve optimal treatment outcomes.
