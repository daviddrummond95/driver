Optimizing Dosing for Quality of Life

As a healthcare professional, you understand the importance of balancing treatment efficacy with patient quality of life. Oncozen is designed to provide a more manageable dosing schedule, allowing patients to focus on what matters most.

Dosing Regimen Frequency
Initial Dose Once every 3 weeks
Maintenance Dose Once every 4 weeks

By optimizing the dosing schedule, Oncozen helps minimize treatment-related disruptions and allows patients to maintain their daily routines. This focus on quality of life is at the core of our commitment to supporting patients and their loved ones throughout their cancer journey.
