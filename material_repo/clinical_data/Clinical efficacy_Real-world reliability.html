Real-World Reliability in Clinical Practice

Oncozen has demonstrated robust clinical efficacy in real-world settings, mirroring the results seen in clinical trials.

  • Improved overall response rate (ORR) in patients with advanced solid tumors
  • Enhanced progression-free survival (PFS) in patients with metastatic disease

These real-world findings reinforce the clinical efficacy of Oncozen, providing confidence in its use for patients with advanced cancer.
