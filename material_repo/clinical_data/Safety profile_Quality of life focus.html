Safety Profile of Oncozen: A Focus on Quality of Life

As a healthcare professional, you understand the importance of balancing treatment efficacy with patient safety and quality of life. Oncozen is committed to providing a favorable safety profile that supports your patients' overall well-being.

  • Low incidence of severe adverse events (<5%)

  • No reported cases of treatment-related mortality

  • Minimal impact on daily activities and functional status

Our commitment to quality of life is reflected in the design of our clinical trials, which prioritize patient-centered outcomes and symptom management. By focusing on the whole patient, not just the disease, we aim to improve treatment experiences and enhance overall well-being.
